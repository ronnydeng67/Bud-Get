Car Picker
Car Picker is a web application that provide car suggestions based on your preferences.

- Start with use the sliders to set your preferences on how much horsepower, fuel economy, budget on your car, also seats capacity.
- Then you can use the selects to choose your preferred body style and transmission.
- Finally choose whether you want your car to be all wheel drive or electrical.
You will see two suggrested cars that match or similar to your preferences with pictures after you press the "Show me cars" button
